Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Per Serving : 146 calories; protein 4.5g; carbohydrates 31.1g; fat 1.4g; cholesterol 3.7mg; sodium 44.9mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 0.5 cup yogurt
  • 1.5 tsp white sugar
  • 0.25 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 tsp orange juice
  • 1 tsp milk

Steps

  1. Blend the banana, yogurt, sugar, pineapple juice, strawberries, orange juice, and milk in a blender until smooth.
