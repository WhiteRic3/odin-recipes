Protein Virgin Pina Colada

Per Serving: 268 calories; protein 38.4g; carbohydrates 25.5g; fat 1.4g; cholesterol 12.5mg; sodium 233.4mg

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen pienapple chunks
  • 1 cup pineapple-coconut sparkling water
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon coconut extract

Steps

  1. Combine pineapple, sparkling water, vanilla protein powder, and coconut extract in a blender; blend until smooth.
